Conway's Game of Life

Grid Size: 40
Speed: 10

How does it work?

Conway's Game of Life is a zero player simulation where cells on a grid live or die based on four simple rules:

1. Cells with < 2 alive neighbors die from isolation
2. Cells with 2 or 3 alive neighbors survive
3. Cells with > 3 alive neighbors die from overpopulation
4. Dead Cells with exactly 3 alive neighbors come alive
